Evalwer

Examnination Portal With Automatic Evalation System

About Us

Evalwer is a student-teacher portal with features like uploading questions, answers, automatic answer evaluation. Students and Teacher can login in the portal and can experience interactive classroom experience online. One of the features of evalwer is its student managing abilities. Teachers can easily manage their students.

Our Services

For Teacher

  1. Teachers can add student in their class through Add Student option.
  2. After adding students in their class, Teacher can also edit their class students marks, if required.
  3. Teachers can upload questions through upload questions options.
  4. Teacher can check their student's assignment submission and can see marks given by evalwer.
  5. Students can message Teachers directly and their messages can be seen in Inbox.
  6. Teacher can also all the marks given by them to a student by accessing their Marks profile and can edit their marks.
  7. User can add profile pic through edit profile option.

For Student

  1. Students can login in the portal and can upload answers to questions uploaded.
  2. Students can download their assignments in the Assignments option and can also submit it their. ( Once assignment is submitted, it can't be changed later. )
  3. Students can contact any teacher through message option in All Teachers List.
  4. Students can check their marks in the Marks option.
  5. User can add profile pic through edit profile option.
